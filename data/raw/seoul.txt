# Seoul Travel Guide

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a dynamic city blending traditional culture with cutting-edge technology.

## Understand
Seoul is one of the world's most developed cities. It's famous for technology, fashion, K-pop, and Korean cuisine. The city is efficient and safe.

## Get in
Incheon International Airport and Gimpo International Airport serve Seoul. Trains connect to other Asian countries. Buses available.

## Get around
The subway system is one of the world's best. Buses are efficient. Taxis are cheap and abundant. Walking is great.

## See
- Gyeongbokgung Palace: Historic royal palace
- Namsan Tower: Tower with city views
- Myeongdong: Shopping district
- Gangnam: Trendy neighborhood
- Bukchon Hanok Village: Traditional Korean houses
- DMZ: Demilitarized zone tour (day trip)
- Cheonggyecheon: Stream through city center

## Do
- Visit palaces and temples
- Shop and explore neighborhoods
- K-pop tours and attractions
- Korean cooking classes
- Visit temples and gardens
- Enjoy nightlife

## Eat
Korean cuisine is diverse and delicious. Try bibimbap, bulgogi, kimchi, Korean BBQ, and street food. Coffee culture strong. Spicy foods common.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $400-800.
