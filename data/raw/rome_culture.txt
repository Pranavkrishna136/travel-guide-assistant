# Rome Safety & Culture

## Safety Guidelines
- Generally safe in tourist areas
- Pickpockets on metro and crowds
- Scams targeting tourists common
- Use official taxis or apps
- Avoid certain areas at night
- Keep valuables secure
- Phone snatching possible
- Unofficial guides to avoid

## Cultural Information
- Ancient Roman heritage
- Catholic faith important
- Artistic masterpieces everywhere
- Passionate people and culture
- Food culture deeply rooted
- Family values central
- Siesta tradition still common
- History in every corner

## Etiquette & Customs
- Greetings often with cheek kiss
- Formal in business settings
- Respect for Catholic sites
- Appropriate dress in churches
- Loud conversation normal
- Gesturing common in speech
- Personal space smaller
- Dress well (fashion important)

## Dining Customs
- Breakfast light (cappuccino, pastry)
- Lunch 1-3pm main meal
- Dinner typically 8-10pm late
- Pasta first course tradition
- Meat or fish second course
- No rushing through meals
- Espresso after dinner
- Leaving cheese on plate rude

## Fashion & Dress
- Italy fashion capital
- Dress stylishly but comfortably
- Covered shoulders in churches
- Long pants preferred over shorts
- Comfortable walking shoes essential
- Layers for temperature changes

## Church Etiquette
- Remove hats and sunglasses
- Dress modestly (covered knees/shoulders)
- Silence and respect necessary
- Photography often restricted
- Genuflect if Catholic
- Don't intrude during mass

## Markets & Shopping
- Local markets lively and colorful
- Campo de' Fiori: Historic square market
- Portobello antique market
- Haggling not expected
- Many shops close Sundays
- Siesta hours 1-4pm

## Entertainment
- Opera and classical music
- Theater productions
- Street performers and musicians
- Fountain serenades
- Piazza gatherings
- Evening passeggiata (stroll)

## Safety Neighborhoods
- Centro Storico: Safe for tourists
- Trevi Fountain area: Crowded but safe
- Colosseum area: Main tourist zone
- Vaticano: Safe and organized
- Trastevere: Charming and lively
- Avoid: Termini station areas, outer suburbs
