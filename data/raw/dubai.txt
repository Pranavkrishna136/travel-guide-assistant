# Dubai Travel Guide

Dubai is a cosmopolitan city in the United Arab Emirates, known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and desert landscapes.

## Understand
Dubai is the most populous city in the UAE. It's a global hub for business, tourism, and finance. The city is known for its extravagance, innovative architecture, and year-round sunshine. It's a melting pot of cultures.

## Get in
Dubai International Airport is the world's busiest airport for international travel. The city is well-connected by air and road.

## Get around
The Dubai Metro is modern and efficient. Taxis are affordable. Rent a car to explore the desert. Walking is good in developed areas like Downtown Dubai and the Marina.

## See
- Burj Khalifa: World's tallest building with observation decks
- Dubai Mall: One of the world's largest shopping malls
- Palm Jumeirah: Man-made palm-shaped island with luxury resorts
- Dubai Fountain: Spectacular fountain shows in a lake
- Old Dubai & Gold Souk: Historic area with traditional markets
- Jumeirah Beach: Beautiful sandy beach
- Dubai Desert: Desert safaris with dunes and Bedouin camps

## Do
- Shop at luxury malls and traditional souks
- Experience desert safari with dune bashing
- Visit theme parks: IMG Worlds, Ferrari World
- Enjoy water sports and beach activities
- Take a dhow cruise on Dubai Creek

## Eat
Dubai has world-class dining. Try Middle Eastern cuisine, Indian, Thai, and international restaurants. Street food and food courts are affordable.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $800-2000 depending on accommodation.
