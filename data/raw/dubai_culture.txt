# Dubai Safety & Culture

## Safety Guidelines
- One of world's safest cities
- Low crime rate for tourists
- Strict laws enforced
- Public drunkenness illegal
- Dress codes in religious areas
- Respect Islamic customs
- LGBTQ+ restrictions exist
- Construction accidents possible

## Cultural Information
- Islamic traditions strong
- Ramadan fasting month observed
- Respect for traditions important
- Business-oriented culture
- Luxury and consumption focus
- Family values important
- Diverse expat population
- Modern Emirati culture blend

## Dress Code
- Women: Modest dress recommended
- Cover shoulders and knees
- Swimwear at beach/pools
- Conservative in religious areas

## Customs & Etiquette
- Right hand for greetings and eating
- Remove shoes in homes
- Respect personal space
- Photography of people requires permission
- Avoid left-hand eating
- Hospitality important and generous

## Dining Variety
- Middle Eastern cuisine
- South Asian cuisine abundant
- International restaurants everywhere
- Alcohol available (special venues)
- Halal meat common
- Fresh seafood and meats
- Date palms and Arabic coffee traditional

## Shopping
- Tax-free shopping advantage
- Gold and spice souks
- Modern mega-malls
- Designer boutiques
- Haggling in traditional markets
- Electronics competitive prices

## Transportation Safety
- Driving standards high
- Harsh penalties for violations
- Speeding enforcement strict
- Taxis safe and regulated
- No drink driving tolerance
- Pedestrians must use crossings

## Business Culture
- Punctuality important
- Formal greetings customary
- Business cards exchanged
- Hierarchy respected
- Negotiations common
- Patience valued
