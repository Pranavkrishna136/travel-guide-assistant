# Ho Chi Minh City Travel Guide

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly Saigon, is Vietnam's largest city known for markets, museums, and vibrant nightlife.

## Understand
Ho Chi Minh City is the economic center of Vietnam. The city is modern, fast-paced, and bustling. It blends old and new architecture throughout.

## Get in
Tan Son Nhat International Airport is the main gateway. Buses and trains connect to other cities. Ferries to Mekong Delta.

## Get around
Taxis and ride-sharing apps common. Motorbikes for rent. Buses available. Walking in District 1.

## See
- War Remnants Museum: History of Vietnam War
- Independence Palace: Presidential palace, architectural marvel
- Notre-Dame Cathedral: French colonial cathedral
- Saigon Central Post Office: Historic colonial building
- Ben Thanh Market: Famous covered market
- Bitexco Financial Tower: Tallest building with views
- Mekong Delta: Waterways and floating villages (day trip)
- Cu Chi Tunnels: Historic war tunnels (day trip)

## Do
- Visit war history sites
- Explore markets
- Take Mekong Delta tours
- Visit Cu Chi Tunnels
- Enjoy nightlife and clubs
- Photography tours
- Relax at cafes and restaurants

## Eat
Ho Chi Minh cuisine features fresh ingredients. Try banh mi, pho, spring rolls, and seafood. Street food abundant and cheap. Coffee culture strong.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $200-400.
