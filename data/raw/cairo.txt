# Cairo Travel Guide

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is the gateway to ancient Egyptian civilization and home to the Great Pyramids.

## Understand
Cairo is one of the oldest cities in the world and one of Africa's largest. It's the center of Egyptian culture and history. The city is bustling and vibrant.

## Get in
Cairo International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect to other Egyptian cities. Buses available for regional travel.

## Get around
The metro system is cheap and efficient. Buses are abundant but crowded. Taxis and ride-sharing available. Walking in certain areas.

## See
- Great Pyramids of Giza: Ancient wonders of the world
- Sphinx: Iconic statue with lion's body
- Egyptian Museum: World's largest collection of Egyptian antiquities
- Khan el-Khalili: Historic bazaar and market
- Cairo Citadel: Historic fortress with mosques
- Coptic Cairo: Historic Christian neighborhood
- National Museum of Egyptian Civilization: New museum

## Do
- Explore pyramids and sphinx
- Visit museums
- Shop in Khan el-Khalili
- Take Nile river cruises
- Enjoy traditional performances
- Visit nearby Saqqara

## Eat
Egyptian cuisine features bread, beans, and meat. Try koshari, falafel, ful medames, and kofta. Street food excellent and cheap. Tea and coffee strong.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $200-400.
