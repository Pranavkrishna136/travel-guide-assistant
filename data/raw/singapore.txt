# Singapore Travel Guide

Singapore is a city-state and one of the most developed countries in Southeast Asia. Known for cleanliness, efficiency, and multicultural diversity.

## Understand
Singapore is a small but mighty nation where East meets West. It's ultra-modern with excellent infrastructure. The city is multicultural with Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western influences.

## Get in
Changi Airport is one of the world's best airports. Trains and buses connect to Malaysia and other regions. Ferries to Indonesia islands.

## Get around
The MRT (metro) system is excellent, efficient, and clean. Buses are comprehensive. Taxis are available. Walking is great in many areas.

## See
- Marina Bay Sands: Iconic hotel and observation deck
- Singapore Gardens by the Bay: Futuristic garden with light shows
- Sentosa Island: Resort island with attractions
- Merlion: Statue symbolizing Singapore
- National Museum: History and culture exhibits
- Chinese Garden: Traditional classical garden
- Arab Quarter: Historic district with mosques and markets

## Do
- Experience futuristic attractions
- Visit hawker centers for street food
- Explore diverse neighborhoods
- Relax on Sentosa Island
- Shop on Orchard Road
- Visit art galleries

## Eat
Multicultural cuisine with Chinese, Malay, and Indian influences. Try laksa, chicken rice, satay, and durian. Hawker centers offer cheap, delicious food.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $600-1200.
