# Boracay Travel Guide

Boracay, Philippines, is a world-famous beach island known for white-sand beaches and water sports.

## Understand
Boracay is the Philippines' premier beach destination. The island is relatively small but densely developed. Known for resort culture and beach activities.

## Get in
Godofredo P. Ramos Airport (Caticlan) serves Boracay. Ferries from Caticlan port. Flights from Manila and other cities.

## Get around
Tricycles are main transport. Walking along beach. Motorbikes for rent. Boracay is small and walkable.

## See
- White Beach: Famous wide sandy beach
- Bulabog Beach: Windy beach for water sports
- Coral reef: Snorkeling and diving
- Willy's Rock: Small island rock formation
- Mount Luho: Hiking trail with views
- D'Mall: Shopping and dining
- Boat watching at sunset

## Do
- Beach relaxation and swimming
- Water sports: parasailing, jet ski, windsurfing
- Scuba diving and snorkeling
- Island hopping tours
- Sunset boat cruises
- Nightlife and beach parties
- Spa and massage

## Eat
Island cuisine features fresh seafood. Try grilled fish, shrimp, squid, and sinigang. Beach restaurants. International cuisine available.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
