# Phuket Travel Guide

Phuket, Thailand's largest island, is a beach destination known for beaches, water sports, and nightlife.

## Understand
Phuket is a major beach resort. The island offers beaches, water activities, and entertainment. It's a popular destination for Thai holidays and tourism.

## Get in
Phuket International Airport serves the island. Ferries connect to other Thai islands. Buses available.

## Get around
Taxis and tuk-tuks are common. Motorbike rentals cheap. Songthaews (shared trucks) available for local travel.

## See
- Patong Beach: Popular beach with entertainment
- Big Buddha: 45-meter tall statue on hilltop
- Phang Nga Bay: Limestone cliffs and islands
- Promthep Cape: Scenic viewpoint for sunset
- Old Phuket Town: Historic buildings and restaurants
- Similan Islands: Island hopping and diving
- Coral reefs for snorkeling

## Do
- Beach activities and swimming
- Water sports: diving, snorkeling, jet skiing
- Island hopping tours
- Thai massage and spa
- Muay Thai training
- Nightlife and clubs
- Elephant sanctuaries

## Eat
Thai cuisine is spicy and flavorful. Try pad thai, green curry, tom yum soup, fresh seafood, and mango sticky rice. Street food cheap and delicious.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
