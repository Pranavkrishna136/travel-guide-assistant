# Athens Travel Guide

Athens, the capital of Greece, is one of the world's oldest cities and cradle of Western civilization.

## Understand
Athens is steeped in ancient history and classical architecture. The city combines ancient ruins with modern amenities. It's a gateway to Greek islands.

## Get in
Athens International Airport "Eleftherios Venizelos" is the main airport. Ferries connect to Greek islands. Trains connect to rest of Europe.

## Get around
The metro, tram, and bus system is efficient. Taxis available. Walking is great in the center. Ferries to nearby islands.

## See
- Acropolis: Ancient citadel with Parthenon temple
- Parthenon: Ancient Greek temple dedicated to Athena
- Ancient Agora: Archaeological site of ancient marketplace
- National Archaeological Museum: World's largest museum of ancient Greek artifacts
- Temple of Olympian Zeus: Large ruined temple
- Syntagma Square: Central square with Parliament
- Plaka: Historic neighborhood with narrow streets

## Do
- Explore ancient ruins and temples
- Visit museums
- Hike up Acropolis
- Enjoy sunset views
- Visit nearby Delphi
- Take ferry to islands

## Eat
Greek cuisine features olives, feta, olive oil, and seafood. Try moussaka, souvlaki, gyros, spanakopita, and baklava. Greek salad everywhere.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $400-800.
