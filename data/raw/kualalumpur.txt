# Kuala Lumpur Travel Guide

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia's capital, is known for twin towers, modern infrastructure, and diverse culture.

## Understand
Kuala Lumpur is a modern, multicultural city. It blends traditional Southeast Asia with ultra-modern development. The city is efficient, clean, and affordable.

## Get in
Kuala Lumpur International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect to other cities. Buses available.

## Get around
The LRT and monorail system is modern and cheap. Buses extensive. Taxis and ride-sharing apps available.

## See
- Petronas Twin Towers: Iconic twin skyscrapers
- Menara Kuala Lumpur: TV tower with views
- Batu Caves: Hindu temple in limestone cave
- Central Market: Historic market for crafts
- Thean Hou Temple: Oldest Chinese temple
- Merdeka Square: Historic central square
- Klang Valley: Industrial area with attractions
- Sunway Lagoon: Theme park

## Do
- Visit Petronas Towers
- Explore Batu Caves
- Shop and dine
- Experience street food
- Visit temples
- Theme park attractions
- Photography of skyline

## Eat
Malaysian cuisine blends Chinese, Indian, and Malay. Try laksa, satay, nasi lemak, and roti canai. Street food abundant and cheap. Diverse food courts.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
