# Cape Town Travel Guide

Cape Town, South Africa's legislative capital, is known for Table Mountain, beaches, and diverse culture.

## Understand
Cape Town is one of Africa's most developed cities. It blends African, European, and Asian cultures. The city is beautiful, vibrant, and welcoming.

## Get in
O.R. Tambo International Airport (Johannesburg) connects to Cape Town. Flights available. Buses and trains available.

## Get around
Buses and trains available. Taxis (shared minibuses) common. Car rental recommended. Walking in certain areas.

## See
- Table Mountain: Iconic flat-topped mountain
- Cape Point: Southern tip with dramatic cliffs
- Robben Island: Historic prison island, UNESCO site
- V&A Waterfront: Shopping, dining, and entertainment
- District Six Museum: Apartheid history museum
- Constantia Wine Valley: Wine region nearby
- Boulders Beach: Boulder-filled beach with penguins
- Twelve Apostles: Mountains along coast

## Do
- Hike Table Mountain
- Visit Robben Island
- Wine tasting in Constantia Valley
- Cape Point scenic drive
- Beach activities
- Township tours and cultural experiences
- Shark cage diving

## Eat
South African cuisine features meat and local produce. Try biltong, boerewors, mopane worms, and braai (barbecue). Wine excellent from local regions.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $400-800.
