# Bali Travel Guide

Bali, a province of Indonesia, is known for its beaches, temples, rice paddies, and spiritual culture.

## Understand
Bali is a Hindu-majority island in predominantly Muslim Indonesia. It's famous for its natural beauty, art, and spiritual traditions. The island offers everything from beaches to mountains.

## Get in
Ngurah Rai International Airport serves Bali. Fast boats connect to nearby islands. Ferries to Java and Lombok available.

## Get around
Motorbike rentals are cheap and popular. Taxis and ride-sharing apps available. Scooters are the local transport method. Car rentals with drivers common.

## See
- Tanah Lot: Temple on a rock formation in the sea
- Ubud: Cultural heart with art, dance, and crafts
- Tegallalang Rice Terraces: Stunning agricultural landscape
- Bali Swing: Popular suspended swing in the jungle
- Monkey Forest: Sanctuary with playful monkeys
- Mount Batur: Active volcano with hiking trails
- Kuta Beach: Popular beach with sunset views

## Do
- Surfing at various beaches
- Spa and massage treatments (very affordable)
- Visit temples and learn about Balinese culture
- Participate in yoga and meditation
- Explore rice paddies
- Take cooking classes

## Eat
Balinese cuisine is diverse and flavorful. Try nasi goreng, satay, lumpia, and local fruit. Street food is delicious and cheap. Fresh seafood everywhere.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
