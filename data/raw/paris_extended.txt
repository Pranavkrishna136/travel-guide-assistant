# Paris Additional Details

## Neighborhoods
- Montmartre: Artistic neighborhood with Sacré-Cœur
- Le Marais: Trendy area with boutiques and galleries
- Latin Quarter: Student area with history
- Île de la Cité: Historic island with Notre-Dame
- Saint-Germain: Bohemian neighborhood with cafes

## Museums Worth Visiting
- Musée d'Orsay: Impressionist art in former train station
- Centre Pompidou: Contemporary art center
- Musée de l'Orangerie: Water lilies and Impressionist works
- Musée Rodin: Rodin's sculptures and gardens
- Musée Picasso: Extensive Picasso collection

## Day Trips
- Versailles: Opulent palace and gardens
- Fontainebleau: Royal castle and forest
- Mont-Saint-Michel: Medieval abbey on tidal island

## Shopping
- Galeries Lafayette: Department store
- Printemps: Historic department store
- Marais boutiques: Designer and vintage shops
- Les Halles: Modern shopping center

## Dining
- Michelin-starred restaurants throughout
- Casual bistros with traditional French cuisine
- Food markets for fresh produce
- Wine bars for wine tasting

## Transportation
- Paris Visite passes for metro access
- Vélib' bike sharing system popular
- RER trains for day trips

## Best Time to Visit
- Spring (April-May): Pleasant weather, flowers blooming
- Fall (September-October): Perfect weather, fewer crowds
- Winter (December): Christmas markets and decorations
- Summer (July-August): Sunny but crowded and hot
