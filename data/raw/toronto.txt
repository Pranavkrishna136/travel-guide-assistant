# Toronto Travel Guide

Toronto, Canada's largest city, is multicultural and known for diverse neighborhoods, entertainment, and natural attractions.

## Understand
Toronto is one of the world's most multicultural cities. The city is clean, safe, and well-organized. It's a gateway to Niagara Falls.

## Get in
Toronto Pearson International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect to US and other Canadian cities. Buses available.

## Get around
The TTC (metro, streetcar, buses) system is efficient. Taxis and ride-sharing available. Walking is great in many neighborhoods.

## See
- CN Tower: Iconic tower with observation deck
- Niagara Falls: Spectacular waterfall nearby (day trip)
- Toronto Islands: Islands with beaches and parks
- St. Lawrence Market: Historic public market
- Casa Loma: Gothic Revival mansion
- Ontario Museum of Natural History
- Distillery District: Historic neighborhood with shops

## Do
- Visit Niagara Falls
- Explore neighborhoods
- Enjoy live music venues
- Visit museums
- Take ferry to islands
- Shop and dine

## Eat
Toronto has international cuisine from everywhere. Try Canadian poutine, bagels, diverse Asian and European food. Street food abundant.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $600-1200.
