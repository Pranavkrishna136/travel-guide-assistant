# Hanoi Travel Guide

Hanoi, Vietnam's capital, is known for ancient temples, French colonial architecture, and vibrant street life.

## Understand
Hanoi is one of the world's oldest capitals with over 1,000 years of history. The city blends ancient Vietnamese, French colonial, and modern influences. It's chaotic but charming.

## Get in
Noi Bai International Airport serves Hanoi. Trains connect to other Vietnamese cities and China. Buses available.

## Get around
Taxis and ride-sharing apps common. Motorbikes for rent. Buses available. Walking in Old Quarter.

## See
- Hoan Kiem Lake: Central lake with Turtle Tower
- Old Quarter: Historic neighborhood with narrow streets
- Temple of Literature: Vietnam's oldest university
- Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum: Memorial and grounds
- Tran Quoc Pagoda: Oldest temple in Vietnam
- One Pillar Pagoda: Historic wooden temple
- Vietnamese Women's Museum: History and culture
- Long Bien Bridge: Historic colonial bridge

## Do
- Explore Old Quarter streets
- Visit temples and shrines
- Take cyclo rides
- Visit museums
- Enjoy street food tours
- Photography walks
- Relax at cafes

## Eat
Vietnamese cuisine is flavorful and fresh. Try pho, banh mi, spring rolls, and fresh summer rolls. Coffee culture strong. Street food excellent and cheap.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $200-400.
