# San Francisco Travel Guide

San Francisco, California's iconic city, is known for the Golden Gate Bridge, hills, and tech industry.

## Basic Travel Information
San Francisco is a compact, walkable city on a peninsula. Known for steep hills, diverse neighborhoods, and progressive culture. One of America's most visited cities.

## Tourist Attractions
- Golden Gate Bridge: Iconic suspension bridge with views
- Alcatraz Island: Historic prison, now museum
- Cable Cars: Historic transportation and attraction
- Painted Ladies: Colorful Victorian houses
- Fisherman's Wharf: Pier with sea lions and restaurants
- Chinatown: Historic neighborhood with shops
- Golden Gate Park: Large urban park
- Ferry Building Marketplace: Historic marketplace

## Best Time to Visit
- September-October: Perfect weather, less fog
- May-June: Warm and sunny
- July-August: Busy and foggy
- Winter (Nov-Feb): Cool but manageable

## Transportation Options
- Cable cars (iconic)
- BART (rapid transit)
- Buses and streetcars
- Taxis and ride-sharing
- Walking (challenging with hills)
- Ferries to nearby islands

## Cultural Information
- Art galleries and museums
- Live music venues
- LGBTQ+ friendly city
- Tech and innovation culture
- Diverse food scene
- Counter-culture heritage

## Safety Guidelines
- Generally safe in main tourist areas
- Avoid certain neighborhoods after dark
- Watch for pickpockets on public transit
- Car break-ins common, don't leave valuables
- Petty theft in crowded areas
- Use official taxis or ride-sharing apps

## Dining
- Fine dining and casual restaurants
- Asian cuisine excellent (Chinese, Vietnamese, Japanese)
- Fresh seafood at Fisherman's Wharf
- Mission burritos famous
- Coffee culture strong

## Budget
A 3-day trip costs $700-1400.
