# Dubai Extended - Beach Resorts

## Beaches
- Jumeirah Beach: Public beach with clear water
- Kite Beach: Popular for water sports
- Al Mamzar Beach: Local beach with parks

## Shopping & Nightlife
- Dubai Mall: One of world's largest malls
- Gold Souk: Traditional gold market
- Spice Market: Fragrant spice and herb market
- Bastakiya Quarter: Historic merchant district
- Bars and nightclubs: Multiple venues

## Activities
- Desert safaris with dune bashing
- Dune buggy rides
- Camel rides and racing
- Falconry experiences
- Boat cruises and yacht rentals
- Skydiving over Palm Jumeirah
- Water parks and attractions

## Day Trips
- Abu Dhabi: Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Emirates Palace
- Ras Al Khaimah: Mountains and adventure sports
- Hatta: Mountain town with lakes

## Best Time to Visit
- October-April: Pleasant weather (20-30°C)
- November-February: Perfect weather, events
- Summer: Extremely hot (40°C+), less crowded

## Shopping & Markets
- Traditional gold and spice souks
- Modern mega-malls
- Beach clubs and lounges
- Fine dining restaurants

## Cultural Events
- Dubai Shopping Festival
- Dubai World Cup (horse racing)
- Formula E racing
- Art exhibitions and performances
