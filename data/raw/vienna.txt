# Vienna Travel Guide

Vienna, Austria's capital, is known for classical music, palaces, and imperial history.

## Understand
Vienna is one of Europe's most elegant cities. It was the heart of the Austro-Hungarian Empire. The city is refined, cultural, and beautiful.

## Get in
Vienna International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect throughout Europe. Buses available.

## Get around
The U-Bahn, S-Bahn, and tram system is excellent. Buses available. Taxis and ride-sharing apps. Walking in city center.

- Visit palaces and gardens
- Museum visits
- Coffee house culture
- Strauss concerts
- Danube river cruises
- Photography walks

## Eat
Viennese cuisine is hearty and sweet. Try wiener schnitzel, sacher torte, strudel, and goulash. Coffee culture world-famous. Street food available.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $500-1000.
