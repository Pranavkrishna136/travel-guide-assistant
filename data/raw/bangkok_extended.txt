# Bangkok Extended - Day Trips & Activities

## Day Trips
- Ayutthaya: Ancient capital with temples (1 hour north)
- Floating Markets: Ampawa and Damnoen Saduak
- Bridge on River Kwai: Historical WWII site
- Khao Yai National Park: Wildlife and jungle

## Neighborhoods
- Sukhumvit: Upscale dining and nightlife
- Silom: Business district with nightlife
- Sampheng: Fabric and goods market
- Thonburi: Traditional Bangkok on west bank

## Markets
- Chatuchak Weekend Market: Huge market with everything
- Train Market: Market on active railway
- Floating markets: Boats selling goods
- Talad Rod Fai: Antique and retro market

## Activities
- Muay Thai training and fights
- Thai cooking classes
- Thai massage and spa
- Temple visits and meditation
- River cruises and boat tours
- Shopping malls and street markets
- Nightlife and clubs

## Accommodation
- Luxury hotels and resorts
- Mid-range hotels and guesthouses
- Hostels and backpacker lodges
- Traditional Thai houses

## Best Time to Visit
- November-February: Cool and dry
- March-May: Hot and dry
- June-October: Rainy season, fewer tourists
