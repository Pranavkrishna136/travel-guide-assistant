# Marrakech Travel Guide

Marrakech, Morocco's Red City, is known for medinas, riads, and desert proximity.

## Understand
Marrakech is one of Morocco's most visited cities. It blends ancient medinas with modern development. The city is exotic and culturally rich.

## Get in
Marrakech Menara Airport is the main gateway. Buses and trains connect to other cities. Desert access nearby.

## Get around
Walking in medina is primary. Taxis and ride-sharing available. Motorbikes for rent. Horse-drawn carriages.

## See
- Jemaa el-Fnaa Square: Bustling central square with performers
- Koutoubia Mosque: Iconic mosque with minaret
- Medina: Old city with narrow streets and souks
- Bahia Palace: Historic palace with courtyards
- Saadian Tombs: Historic royal tombs
- Ben Youssef Mosque: Historic mosque
- Palmeraie: Palm grove outside city
- Atlas Mountains: Hiking day trips

## Do
- Explore medina and souks
- Visit palaces and tombs
- Watch street performers
- Shop for crafts and carpets
- Visit nearby Kasbahs and Kasbah Ait Benhaddou
- Desert trips and camel rides
- Hammam (bath house) visits

## Eat
Moroccan cuisine is aromatic and flavorful. Try tagine, couscous, pastilla, and fresh mint tea. Street food abundant. Bread and olives everywhere.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $200-400.
