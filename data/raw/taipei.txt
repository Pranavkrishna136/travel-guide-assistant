# Taipei Travel Guide

Taipei, Taiwan's capital, is known for modern architecture, night markets, and technology.

## Understand
Taipei is a modern, cosmopolitan city. It blends traditional Chinese culture with cutting-edge modernity. The city is efficient, clean, and welcoming.

## Get in
Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport is the main gateway. High-speed rail connects to other cities. Buses available.

## Get around
The MRT (metro) system is modern and efficient. Buses comprehensive. Taxis abundant. Biking popular.

## See
- Taipei 101: Iconic skyscraper with observation deck
- Chiang Kai-shek Memorial: Changing of guard ceremony
- National Palace Museum: Chinese art and history
- Shilin Night Market: Famous night market
- Raohe Night Market: Street food night market
- Tamsui Old Street: Historic waterfront
- Elephant Mountain: Hiking with city views
- Jiufen Old Street: Historic shopping area

## Do
- Visit night markets
- Hiking and outdoor activities
- Hot spring bathing in Beitou
- Temple visits
- Photography walks
- Food tours
- Modern architecture tours

## Eat
Taiwanese cuisine features dumplings, noodles, and stinky tofu. Night markets famous for street food. Bubble tea originated here. Street food abundant.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
