# Barcelona Extended

## Neighborhoods
- Gothic Quarter: Medieval old town with narrow streets
- Gràcia: Bohemian neighborhood with plazas
- Eixample: Modernist district with Gaudí buildings
- Montjuïc: Hill with museums and views
- La Rambla: Famous tree-lined pedestrian street

## Attractions
- Sagrada Família: Gaudí's masterpiece basilica
- Park Güell: Colorful park with terraces
- Casa Batlló: Modernist residential building
- La Boqueria Market: Historic market with food
- Montjuïc Castle: Historic fortress with views
- National Art Museum of Catalonia: MNAC

## Activities
- Beach relaxation at Barceloneta
- FC Barcelona football matches
- Gothic Quarter explorations
- Museum visits
- Street performances on La Rambla
- Tapas and wine bars
- Modernist architecture tours

## Best Time
- April-May: Spring, perfect weather
- September-October: Warm, less crowded
- December: Christmas markets
- July-August: Hot, crowded

## Cultural Info
- Catalan culture and language
- Art and design centric
- Passionate about football
- Beach culture important
- Bohemian artistic heritage
- Fiesta and celebration tradition

## Safety
- Generally safe for tourists
- Pickpockets on metro and crowded areas
- Beach theft possible
- Use approved taxis or apps
- Avoid certain neighborhoods after dark
- Watch belongings in crowds
