# London Safety & Culture

## Safety Guidelines
- One of Europe's safest capitals
- Petty theft on public transit
- Pickpockets in crowded areas
- Use contactless or cards
- Don't flash valuables
- Avoid certain neighborhoods at night
- Police visible and helpful
- Emergency number: 999

## Cultural Information
- English heritage and traditions
- Royal family significant
- Diverse multicultural city
- Pub culture important
- Theater district famous
- Museums and culture free
- Tea time tradition
- Reserved but friendly

## Etiquette & Customs
- Queue culture strictly observed
- Politeness and manners important
- Reserved in public
- Humor often sarcastic
- Apologizing common
- Personal space respected
- Formality in business
- Dress casually but neat

## Tea Culture
- Afternoon tea with pastries
- Proper tea service etiquette
- Cups and saucers important
- Tea before milk tradition
- Finger sandwiches included
- Scones with jam and cream
- Popular activity and tradition

## Dining Customs
- Breakfast can be full English
- Lunch midday meal
- Dinner evening meal
- Fish and chips famous
- Sunday roast tradition
- Pub food (pub grub) popular
- Tipping 10-15% customary
- Pubs important social spaces

## Pub Culture
- Central to social life
- Diverse clientele and settings
- Cask ales popular
- Lager, bitter, stout varieties
- Pub games: darts, pool
- Smoking outside only
- Traditional and modern pubs
- Real ale appreciation

## Shopping Culture
- Oxford Street major retail
- Oxford Circus and Regent Street
- Harrods luxury department store
- Borough Market food market
- Portobello Road antiques
- Many shops closed Sundays
- Boxing Day sales major event

## Transportation
- London Underground oldest metro
- Buses comprehensive and efficient
- Pickpockets on peak times
- Oyster cards and contactless
- Walking wonderful in areas
- Black cabs (taxis) reliable
- River Thames cruises scenic

## Museums
- Most major museums free entry
- British Museum: History and antiquities
- National Gallery: Art collection
- Victoria and Albert: Design and culture
- Natural History: Dinosaurs and specimens
- Advance booking for popular museums

## Best Neighborhoods
- Westminster: Historic government center
- Kensington: Upscale and cultural
- Covent Garden: Shopping and theater
- Soho: Entertainment and restaurants
- Shoreditch: Hip and artistic
- Camden: Markets and alternative culture
- Notting Hill: Bohemian and trendy
