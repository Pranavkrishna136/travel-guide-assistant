# Moscow Travel Guide

Moscow, Russia's capital, is known for Red Square, Kremlin, and grand architecture.

## Understand
Moscow is Russia's political and cultural center. It's a massive, vibrant city with impressive architecture. The city is intense and fascinating.

## Get in
Sheremetyevo International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect to other Russian cities and Europe. Buses available.

## Get around
The metro system is one of the world's most beautiful with art and mosaics. Buses and trams available. Taxis and ride-sharing apps.

## See
- Red Square: Historic central square
- St. Basil's Cathedral: Iconic colorful cathedral
- Kremlin: Historic fortress and government center
- Kremlin Walls: Fortress walls with towers
- Bolshoi Theatre: World-famous ballet theatre
- Pushkin Museum: Art museum
- Tretyakov Gallery: Russian art museum
- GUM Store: Historic shopping arcade

## Do
- Explore Red Square and Kremlin
- Attend Bolshoi performances
- Visit museums
- Experience metro stations
- Take river cruises
- Photography tours
- Experience Russian culture

## Eat
Russian cuisine features hearty soups, bread, and meat. Try borscht, pelmeni, stroganoff, and blini. Vodka culture strong. Street food available.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
