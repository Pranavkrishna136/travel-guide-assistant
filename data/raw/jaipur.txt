# Jaipur Travel Guide

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is Rajasthan's capital famous for palatial architecture and vibrant culture.

## Understand
Jaipur is the gateway to Rajasthan. The city is known for its pink-colored buildings and royal heritage. It's organized in a grid pattern planned by Maharaja.

## Get in
Jaipur International Airport is the main gateway. Trains connect to Delhi and other cities. Buses available.

## Get around
Taxis and auto-rickshaws common. Some walking in City Palace area. Bicycles and scooters available for rent.

## See
- City Palace: Still partially royal residence, UNESCO site
- Jantar Mantar: Astronomical observation site, UNESCO site
- Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds): Five-story pink structure with small windows
- Albert Hall Museum: Decorated museum building
- Govind Dev Ji Temple: Historic temple
- Nahargarh Fort: Hilltop fort with views
- Bazaars: Colorful markets selling textiles and handicrafts

## Do
- Explore City Palace
- Visit royal sites
- Shop for handicrafts and textiles
- Take camel rides (outside city)
- Visit temples
- Experience local culture
- Photography tours

## Eat
Rajasthani cuisine is rich and spicy. Try dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, laal maas, and sweet dishes. Street food abundant. Lassi (yogurt drink) popular.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $150-300.
