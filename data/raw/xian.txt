# Xi'an Travel Guide

Xi'an, capital of Shaanxi Province, is home to the Terracotta Army and ancient history.

## Understand
Xi'an is one of China's oldest cities and was the ancient Silk Road hub. The city preserves ancient Chinese civilization. It's less touristy than Beijing or Shanghai.

## Get in
Xi'an Xianyang International Airport serves the city. High-speed trains to Beijing and other cities. Buses available.

## Get around
Metro system exists. Buses are cheap. Taxis and ride-sharing available. Walking in city center.

## See
- Terracotta Army: Ancient clay warriors, UNESCO site
- City Walls: Well-preserved ancient city walls
- Great Mosque: Blend of Islamic and Chinese architecture
- Tang West Market Museum: Historic trade center site
- Shaanxi History Museum: Extensive history museum
- Big Wild Goose Pagoda: Historic Buddhist pagoda
- Small Wild Goose Pagoda: Historic pagoda

## Do
- Visit Terracotta Army
- Walk city walls
- Explore ancient sites
- Visit temples
- Explore night markets
- Photography tours
- Experience local culture

## Eat
Xi'an cuisine is hearty and flavorful. Try roujiamo (meat sandwich), biangbiang noodles, and dumplings. Night markets famous for food. Street food abundant.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $200-400.
