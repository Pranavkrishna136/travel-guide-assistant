# Prague Travel Guide

Prague, Czech Republic's capital, is known for castle, bridges, and medieval architecture.

## Understand
Prague is one of Europe's most beautiful cities. It has been relatively untouched by wars and maintains medieval character. The city is affordable and walkable.

## Get in
Václav Havel Airport Prague is the main gateway. Trains connect to Europe. Buses available.

## Get around
Walking is the best way to explore. Trams and metro available. Taxis and ride-sharing apps.

## See
- Prague Castle: Large castle complex with cathedral
- Charles Bridge: Historic bridge with statues
- Old Town Square: Central historic square with church
- Prague Astronomical Clock: Medieval clock with hourly show
- Petřín Tower: Iron tower like Eiffel Tower
- Jewish Quarter: Historic Jewish neighborhoods
- St. Vitus Cathedral: Gothic cathedral
- Powder Tower: Historic city gate

## Do
- Walk Charles Bridge
- Explore castle complex
- Visit old town
- Enjoy beer culture
- Photography tours
- Visit nearby Cesky Krumlov
- River boat cruises

## Eat
Czech cuisine features meat and beer. Try goulash, tripe soup, schnitzel, and knedle. Beer is world-famous and cheap. Street food available.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $300-600.
