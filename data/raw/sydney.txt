# Sydney Travel Guide

Sydney, the capital of New South Wales, is Australia's largest city and famous for its iconic landmarks and beaches.

## Understand
Sydney is known for its stunning harbor, world-class beaches, and vibrant multicultural lifestyle. It's one of the most livable cities globally with excellent infrastructure and outdoor lifestyle.

## Get in
Sydney Airport is the main international airport. Trains, buses, and cars connect to other Australian cities. Ferries are a scenic way to travel.

## Get around
The metro/train system is efficient and extensive. Buses cover the entire city. Ferries are scenic and fun. Walking is excellent in many areas.

## See
- Sydney Opera House: Iconic performing arts venue, UNESCO site
- Sydney Harbour Bridge: Historic bridge offering panoramic views
- Bondi Beach: Famous sandy beach with great waves
- Blue Mountains: Mountain range with hiking trails
- Taronga Zoo: Zoo with animal shows and harbor views
- Royal Botanic Gardens: Beautiful gardens overlooking harbor
- Manly Beach: Coastal town beach with ocean pools

## Do
- Swim at famous beaches
- Climb the Harbour Bridge
- Take ferry rides in the harbor
- Visit art museums and galleries
- Explore coastal walks
- Relax in beachside cafes

## Eat
Australian cuisine features fresh seafood, meat, and produce. Try fish and chips, meat pie, Vegemite toast, and lamingtons. Coffee culture is strong.

## Budget
A 3-day trip can cost $700-1400.
